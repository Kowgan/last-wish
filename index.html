Last Wish

An open source Ultima Online RunUO Freeshard hoping to fulfill one last wish. Everyone is welcome!

Three Steps to Join

  1. Download UO Client 7.0.15.1 (Installer or Portable)
  2. Download and install UOS
  3. Launch UOS and configure like this:


Shard: lastwish.servegame.com
Port: 2593

Accounts are created automatically on the first login.

If you need any help, look for one of the administrators in game: Victor, Adrian or Elisa.

Shard Features

  • Free and Open Source! Anyone can contribute or use the scrips in their on shards!
  • Total Skill Cap: 1200. Individual Skill Cap: 120. That means you can reach 120 in 10 skills.
  • In-game Forum and Chat, so we don't need to do anything out of the game.
  • Optional Hunger/Thirst System. You loses nothing if you don't use it. But there's a bonnus for those who use.
  • Directional Damage System, so using strategies in group combat really makes difference.
  • Skill Title and Health Status are displayed under the character name.
  • Duel System, allows to duel anywhere, even outside Felucca.
  • Custom start location and meeting point for players.
  • Darker nights and improved time cycle, for better RP.
  • Bloody Bandage System allows to recover bandages that had no effect.
  • Players corpses only decay after 24h.
  • When in party, gold is auto-splited equally among party members.
  • Auto-defend system makes your character automatically counter-attack the last attacker. This feature can be disabled if you want.
  • Kudos System alows players to trade kudos for custom rewards.
  • Lots of custom commands to make the game more pleasurable.
